Chill Kitchen & Bar is a modern American restaurant in the Marlborough area.


Local owners, John and Coleen Logomasini, have created a fun, relaxed atmosphere that provides an excellent wine list, always changing cocktail menu, and craft beers. The concept is simple... clean and fresh food, using simple ingredients with the best quality to create a menu that provides for everyone who wants to dine with us. The decor and service is inviting and makes you feel right at home the second you walk through the door. With live entertainment on the weekends, weekend brunch, and an amazing patio and bar, you can always find something to do with friends and family.

Come enjoy some great food, great drinks... and chill with us.
